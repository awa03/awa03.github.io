Freud's Legacy

Freud has lived on through his theories within academia. Freud has continued to influence philosphers, psychologists, and scientists alike. Philosphers such as Lacan, Marcuse, and Zizek derived many of their theories from his teachings. Freuds ideas are still taught today in many modern universitys, his teachings continue to live on because of it.
