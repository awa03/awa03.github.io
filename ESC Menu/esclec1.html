What is Science?

Science is the use of observation, expirementation, and calculation in order to explain how nature operates. The scientific method is a sequence of steps for analying scientific problems in a way that leads to verifiable results. The scientific method allows us to: find answers to questions, solution to problems, and evidence to support or refute ideas. The first step to the scientific method is observation. In this step we find the problem that needs to be answered. The second step is formulating a hypothesis, this should attempt to explain later observations. You may need multiple working hypothesis within a scientific expirement. The third test is Testing!. When testing your hypothesis there are four rules: 1) Results must be replicable, 2) It must explain all related observations, 3) There must be no contradictions with your observations and your data, 4) You must maintain internally consistent.

Once a hypothesis has passed many tests it may become a theory. A theory explains a broad range of observation and is supported by an abundance of evidence.

The Doppler Effect

Wavelength is the spacing between waves, while frequency is the number of waves passing in an interval of time. Blue light means that the wave has a high frequence, while a red light is signified by a low frequency. The doppler effect occurs when waves compress or relax with relative motion. A stopped train may sound the same to someone on the right and left, however if the train is in motion to the left:
- the left person hears a higher pitch from the compressed sound waves
- the right person hears a lower pitch because of the expanded sound waves
- as the train passes the person on the left the pitch would drop (high to low)

The Doppler effect also applies to visible light. Wave lengths range from 400 to 700 nanometers. Blue waves means that they are of higher frequencies, whereas red waves have lower frequencies. Because of this light from an approching star may shift towards the color blue. This effect has led us to discovering that the universe is exanding and moving away from us, because distant galaxies appeared red shifted.

The Big Bang Theory

All of the mass and energy in the universe was once packed into a single small point. In ~14 G.A it exploded and has been expanding ever since. The 1st atoms were hydrogen and helium, and they were developed soon after the big bang.

Edwin Hubble discovered that distant gaalaxies are uniformly red shifted, rather than blue shifted. This means that they are moving away from us.

The universe started out at ~100% hydrogen and helium. Nuecleosythesis occured at the earliest stages of the big band. This is why the present composition is ~90% H ~10% HE <<1% heavier elements After the big bang there was cooling and expansion. Hydrogen turned into h2 - the fuel of stars. Gravity caused the collapse of gaseous nebula, resulting in increases i: temperature, denisty, and Rate of rotation. Condensed nebulae formed accertion discs. Heat and mass collapse ignited nuclear fusion as the stars became h2 starved, the initiated: collapse and heating, heavy element production, catastrophic explosions (supernova).

Nucleosynthesis

Stars are element factories. The big bangs nucleosythesis formed lighter elements. The havier elements are formed from stellar nucleosynthesis. Atomic #s > 26 are formed during supernovae. The smaller-mass stars burn more slowly and live longer. They create lighter elements up to carbon. Larger-mass stars burn rapidly, and live shorter. They create elements up to iron.
